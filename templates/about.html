Instructions:

Welcome to our Art Classification Web Page!

Step 0:

Click the green BEGIN button to start!

Step 1: Upload an Image

First you need to select an image that is either a jpg or jpeg file type. To upload this image onto the web app, either select the green UPLOAD button or drag and drop the image from another location. Then, verify that the shown image is the picture of your selected image that you want to identify. If not, simply press the green UPLOAD button again to select another image.

Step 2: Select classification category/categories

Now you may select any combination of the following 3 categories to identify your image with:

1. Style: the possible period of art such as realism, impressionism, etc.
The 22 styles available in this classifier include: Abstract Art, Abstract Expressionism, Art Informel, Art Nouveau (Modern), Baroque, Cubism, Early Renaissance, Expressionism, High Renaissance, Impressionism, Magic Realism, Mannerism (Late Renaissance), Naïve Art (Primitivism), Neoclassicism, Northern Renaissance, Post-Impressionism, Realism, Rococo, Romanticism, Surrealism, Symbolism, and Ukiyo-e.

2. Time period: the possible start and end date the art would likely be created in based on style.

3. Artist: the possible creator.
The 127 artists available in this classifier include: Albert Bierstadt, Albrecht Durer, Alfred Sisley, Alphonse Mucha, Amedeo Modigliani, Arkhip Kuindzhi, Aubrey Beardsley, Berthe Morisot, Boris Kustodiev, Camille Corot, Camille Pissarro, Charles M. Russell, Charles Turner, Childe Hassam, Claude Monet, Dante Gabriel Rossetti, David Burliuk, Edgar Degas, Edouard Cortes, Edouard Manet, Egon Schiele, Ernst Ludwig Kirchner, Erte, Eugene Boudin, Eugene Delacroix, Eyvind Earle, Felix Vallotton, Ferdinand Hodler, Fernand Leger, Francisco Goya, Gene Davis, George Romney, George Stefanescu-Ramnic, Georges Braque, Georgia O'Keeffe, Giorgio de Chirico, Giovanni Battista Piranesi, Giovanni Boldini, Gustave Caillebotte, Gustave Courbet, Gustave Dore, Gustave Loiseau, Gustave Moreau, Henri Fantin-Latour, Henri Martin, Henri Matisse, Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec, Hieronymus Bosch, Honore Daumier, Ilya Mashkov, Ilya Repin, Isaac Levitan, Ivan Aivazovsky, Ivan Bilibin, Ivan Shishkin, James McNeill Whistler, James Tissot, Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, Jean-Leon Gerome, Joan Miro, John Henry Twachtman, John James Audubon, John Singer Sargent, Joshua Reynolds, Juan Gris, Katsushika Hokusai, Koloman Moser, Konstantin Korovin, Konstantin Makovsky, Konstantin Somov, Konstantin Yuon, Kuzma Petrov-Vodkin, Louis Comfort Tiffany, Lovis Corinth, Lucas Cranach the Elder, Lucian Freud, M.C. Step 3:

After a short period of time, your selected categories will be displayed for your selected image.
